About Chirag Dodiya

I'm a senior software engineer focused on building reliable, scalable backend and distributed systems for the modern web. My core strengths lie in designing fault-tolerant services, optimizing architectures for real-world performance, and breaking down complex problems into simple, maintainable solutions.

Driven by values of reliability and simplicity, I believe great engineering is about empowering teams and businesses by delivering systems people can trust. I enjoy mentoring teammates and collaborating across disciplines, ensuring that every piece of software creates practical, tangible impact.

Whether scaling cloud infrastructure, automating developer workflows, or supporting high-uptime SaaS, I aim to combine robust engineering with clear, human-first communication.
